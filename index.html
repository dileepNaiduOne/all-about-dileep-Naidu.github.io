AI Drive X-ray Baggage Scanner .

X-ray baggage scanners are used to scan the luggage bags at airports and railway station. Generally, these kinds of machines use X-rays to scan the luggage bag. When the luggage bag is passed into the scanner the X-rays are emitted and allowed to pass through the bag these rays are reflected back and shown as an image on the computer screen. The human needs to sit in front of the computer and should check the X-ray images of each and every bag and say whether there are any harmful items in the baggage or not. The main aim of this project is to eliminate the human and train the computer in such a way that it should check the X-ray image for harmful items and should intimate humans. Because Humans undergo fatigue and they can't continuously work under extreme conditions.

AI Drive X-ray Baggage Scanner .

X-ray baggage scanners are used to scan the luggage bags at airports and railway station. The human needs to sit in front of the computer and should check the X-ray images of each and every bag and say whether there are any harmful items in the baggage or not. The main aim of this project is to eliminate the human and train the computer in such a way that it should check the X-ray image for harmful items and should intimate humans. Because Humans undergo fatigue and they can't continuously work under extreme conditions.

Fordon - An Automated Wheelchair

This wheelchair is designed for Hemiplegic and Paraplegic patients. The patient can operate the chair with just two buttons. This device is mainly used to transfer the patients from wheelchair to bed and vice-versa without any caretakers or human assistance. This modern wheelchair works with batteries. This is cost-effective.

Kova Making Machine(KMM)

This Project got us top 1 at IIT Madras, Mechanica. I hope you love Kova sweet, I too love it. But, there is a lot of human effort behind a tasty kova sweet. A person should continuously keep on stirring the milk for 20min - 30min to make kova. It is a really horrible job. So, we automated this process by using KMM. We made this machine in 20days. It is simple to use, it adjustable, it is robust, it is easy to assemble and disassemble and it is easy to clean.
